KiteTrace Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 7, 2026  |  Last updated: April 7, 2026

KiteTrace ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application that helps users trace geometric shapes in real life using GPS. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use KiteTrace:

  • Account Information: Email address and username when you register.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates while you are actively tracing a shape. Location is only accessed while the app is in use (foreground). We do not track your location in the background.
  • Activity Data: Shape traces, session results, accuracy scores, distance traveled, speed, jump height, and calorie estimates generated during tracing sessions.
  • Device Sensor Data: Accelerometer readings used solely for jump detection during active sessions. This data is processed on-device and is not stored or transmitted.
  • Profile Photos: Optional photos you upload to your profile or attach to session records.
  • Usage Data: Basic interaction data collected automatically by Firebase (see Section 4).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the KiteTrace app and its features.
  • To save your shapes and session history across devices.
  • To display leaderboards and social features (following, session sharing).
  • To calculate scores, calorie estimates, and activity statistics.
  • To maintain your account and preferences.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage), which is hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Data is protected by Firebase security rules that restrict access so only you can read or modify your own data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Your GPS coordinates are stored as part of session records so you can review your traced paths. You can delete any session or shape at any time from within the app.

4. Third-Party Services

KiteTrace uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage) — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps (react-native-maps) — Used to display maps within the app — Google Privacy Policy
  • Expo / EAS — Used for app distribution and updates — Expo Privacy Policy

5. Location Permissions

KiteTrace requests foreground location access only ("While Using the App"). Location is used exclusively to guide you along a shape path during an active tracing session. We do not access your location when the app is closed or running in the background.

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings. Without location access, real GPS tracing will not function, but simulation mode remains available.

6. Children's Privacy

KiteTrace is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you (available within the app).
  • Delete your shapes, sessions, and account data at any time from within the app.
  • Request complete account deletion by contacting us (see Section 9).
  • Withdraw location permission at any time via device settings.

8. Data Retention

Your account data, shapes, and sessions are retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
